Volunteer Shift Scheduler

Add volunteers with their availability, list the open shifts you need covered, then match them in one click. Anything that can't be filled gets flagged clearly so you know where to recruit.

Volunteers

Give each person a name, mark any special skill they have, and add the day/time blocks they're free.

Open Shifts

List each role you need to staff, the day/time block, how many spots, and whether it requires the driver skill.
Availability and shifts are grouped into day/time blocks (Morning 9am–12pm, Afternoon 1pm–5pm, Evening 5pm–8pm) rather than exact times, so matching is fast and unambiguous. Each volunteer is placed in at most one shift per run.
